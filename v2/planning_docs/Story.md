# Story

## Rise to Glory / Zero to Hero / Stank to Dank

- Start off on bicycle...
- Then Scooter
- Then Slow motorcycle
- Then Stunt motorcycle
- Then OP/best motorcycle
- Progress from bicycle to scooter to dirt bike to sport bike
  - Be the best racer in town 😎
  - Start on crappy races
  - Upgrade bike
  - Do harder races
    - Need to do tricks to get NOS, needed to win
  - Get golds / trophies

### Prologue
- Start game with cutscene, leading into bicycle gameplay
- Can ride around, can do crappy wheelies
- 1st mission is to deliver newspapers
- You see a group of others stunting on motorcycles, and go watch (cutscene)
- 2nd mission you learn how to do proper wheelies
- Stunt battle w/ bicycles @ skatepark
- 3rd mission you do a street race
